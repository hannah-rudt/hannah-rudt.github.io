Background

Loneliness plagues young adults. A 2021 study conducted by The Cigna Group found that 79% of adults aged 18 to 24 reported feeling lonely. Times of transition, such as starting college or moving to a new location post-graduation, can be especially isolating. Photo Bucket aims to alleviate this by incentivizing young adults to explore "bucket list" experiences in their new environments. These activities are captured and shared with friends.
